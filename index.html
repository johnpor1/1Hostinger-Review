What is Hostinger and Is It the Right Web Host for You?

Hostinger's Background and Mission

Hostinger is a web hosting company that started in Lithuania back in 2004. Now, they have a presence in 150 countries. Their main goal is to offer great, yet affordable web hosting and other services. Hostinger values its users and even has a public roadmap for feedback.

Why is Hostinger Popular?

Hostinger's popularity stems from its competitive pricing and transparent policies. They offer more storage space than competitors like SiteGround and HostGator, and they don't upsell unnecessary services.

Who is Hostinger For?

Hostinger is ideal for beginners, bloggers, students, and small businesses seeking affordable and reliable web hosting solutions.
